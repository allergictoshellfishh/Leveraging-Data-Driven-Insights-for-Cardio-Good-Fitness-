# Leveraging-Data-Driven-Insights-for-Cardio-Good-Fitness-
Using jupyter, created graphs and dashboards for business insights.

 Through data preparation, exploration and visualisations, this project aims to transform raw data into actionable insights. The focus is on answering critical business questions, such as identifying key customer segments, understanding the impact of fitness levels on treadmill selection, and exploring regional variations in sales. The insights gained will support Cardio Good Fitness in making data-driven decisions to support business growth, strategic decision-making, and customer experience enhancement.
